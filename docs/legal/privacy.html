Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

Service Description

Edge Inference at Scale ("the Service") is a technology demonstration by Red Hat and Intel that provides an SMS-based AI assistant for conference attendees. When you text our number, your message is processed by an AI model running at the edge to provide helpful conference information.

Information We Collect

When you use the Service, we collect:

  • Your phone number (to send replies)
  • The content of your SMS messages
  • Timestamps of messages sent and received

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Process your questions and provide responses
  • Monitor service health and performance
  • Improve the demo experience

Mobile Number Privacy

We do not sell, share, or distribute your mobile phone number to any third parties. Your phone number is used exclusively for responding to your SMS inquiries within this service.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your usage. You will only receive messages in direct response to SMS messages you send to us. We do not send unsolicited marketing messages.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Please consult your mobile carrier for details on your messaging plan.

Data Retention

Message data is retained only for the duration of the conference event and is deleted within 30 days after the event concludes. Messages are processed locally on edge hardware and are not stored in cloud services.

Opting Out

You can stop receiving messages at any time by texting STOP to our number. You will receive a confirmation message and no further messages will be sent. Text HELP for assistance.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact the demo team at the Red Hat booth or email the event organizers.

This service is a technology demonstration by Red Hat, Inc. and Intel Corporation.
